Who We Are

A Community School with a 28-Year Legacy

Bangladesh School Muscat (BSM) has been a cornerstone institution for the Bangladeshi expatriate community in Oman since its founding on November 3, 1996. Established under the sponsorship of the Bangladesh Embassy in Oman, BSM was created to meet the growing educational needs of Bangladeshi students living in the Sultanate.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of students, offering a curriculum that blends Bangladeshi heritage with internationally recognized standards, including Edexcel qualifications.
