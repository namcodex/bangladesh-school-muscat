About BSM

A Legacy of Learning Since 1996

Bangladesh School Muscat (BSM) has been a pivotal institution for the Bangladeshi expatriate community in Oman since its inception. Established on November 3, 1996, under the sponsorship of the Bangladesh Embassy in Oman, the school was a response to the growing need for a dedicated educational institution that could cater to Bangladeshi students in the Sultanate of Oman.

BSM offers a curriculum based on the British educational system, providing education from Kindergarten to Grade 12. The school follows a bilingual model — English as the medium of instruction, with Bangla taught as a core subject, ensuring students remain proficient in their mother tongue while prepared for global opportunities.

Our School Facilities

BSM provides comprehensive facilities ensuring a conducive and inspiring learning environment for every student, teacher, and staff member.

Facility 01

Computer Laboratory

Modern systems with updated software — programming, applications, and internet research supporting the full IT curriculum.

Facility 02

Science Laboratories

Dedicated Biology, Chemistry, and Physics labs for hands-on experimentation and practical scientific discovery.

Facility 03

Sports Facilities

Courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, plus grounds for cricket and football — building teamwork and fitness.

Facility 04

Multipurpose Hall

Equipped with stage and sound systems for cultural programs, assemblies, and student performances.

Facility 05

Classrooms & Library

Spacious, well-lit classrooms with modern teaching aids and a comprehensive library with books and digital resources.

Facility 06

Kids Zone & Support Rooms

Kids Zone, medical room, prayer room, canteen, bookstore, and clean sanitation facilities throughout campus.
