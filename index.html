About BSM

A Legacy of Learning Since 1996

Bangladesh School Muscat (BSM) has been a pivotal institution for the Bangladeshi expatriate community in Oman since its inception. Established on November 3, 1996, under the sponsorship of the Bangladesh Embassy in Oman, the school was a response to the growing need for a dedicated educational institution that could cater to Bangladeshi students in the Sultanate of Oman. It received approval from the Omani Ministry of Education.

BSM offers a curriculum based on the British educational system, providing education from Kindergarten to Grade 12. The school follows a bilingual model — English as the medium of instruction, with Bangla taught as a core subject, ensuring students remain proficient in their mother tongue while prepared for global opportunities.

Campus & Resources

Our School Facilities

To ensure a conducive learning environment, BSM provides comprehensive facilities serving the needs of students, teachers, and staff across all levels.

Facility 01

Computer Laboratory

Modern systems with updated software supporting the IT curriculum — programming languages, software applications, and internet research techniques.

Facility 02

Science Laboratories

Dedicated Biology, Chemistry, and Physics labs equipped with advanced instruments for hands-on experimentation under teacher supervision.

Facility 03

Sports Facilities

Courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, plus grounds for cricket and football — fostering fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition among students.

Facility 04

Multipurpose Hall

A versatile space for cultural programs, assemblies, and school events — equipped with stage and sound systems for showcasing student talent.

Facility 05

Classrooms & Library

Spacious, well-lit classrooms with modern teaching aids and a comprehensive library housing books, journals, and digital resources for academic growth.

Facility 06

Kids Zone & Support Rooms

A dedicated Kids Zone for younger students, plus a medical room, prayer room, canteen, bookstore, and clean sanitation facilities throughout the campus.
